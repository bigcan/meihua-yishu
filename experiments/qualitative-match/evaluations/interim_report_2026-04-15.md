## Standout hits (new batch)

### E37 — Grand National (Real=5, Control=1)
Real hexagram 屯→隨 line 4: **"乘馬班如，求婚媾，往吉"** — *"riding horses in formation, seeking alliance, advancing is auspicious."* In a literal horse race. The line text mentions horses. The narrative talks about "coordinated movement through chaotic conditions" and "the quest for partnership as the key to forward movement." The real event: I Am Maximus, ridden by Paul Townend for Willie Mullins, regained his title — first horse since Red Rum (1974) to do so. Mullins also became first trainer since Vincent O'Brien in the 1950s to win three straight. The "emergence from chaos / following alliance" theme maps cleanly onto the comeback narrative.

### E28 — Masters Golf (Real=5, Control=2)

Historic — joining Nicklaus, Faldo, Woods as the only back-to-back Masters winners. The "decided at the final extremity / record-breaking but precarious / withered poplar sprouting flowers" imagery matches the drama precisely.

### E25 — Miami Open (Real=5, Control=2)

## Method comparison — where is the signal coming from?

Breaking the 37 scored events down by casting method tells an important story:

| Method | Events scored | Mean real | Mean ctrl | Diff | Win record |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Batch 1 — Mechanical (字數→數字) | 12 | 3.33 | 2.83 | +0.50 | 7–5 |
| Batch 2 — 取象法 via plain LLM | 20 | 3.55 | 2.55 | **+1.00** | 13–6–1 |
| Batch 3 — 取象法 via full `/meihua-yishu` skill | 5 | 3.60 | 3.00 | +0.60 | 3–2 |

**Key observations**:

1. **The mechanical method (batch 1) is the weakest.** Counting characters to pick trigrams performs only modestly above random.
2. **The imagery-based method (batch 2) is the strongest so far.** Reading what an event is *about* and picking trigrams whose semantic associations match the situation produces a clear +1.00 lead with 13–6 wins.
3. **The full-skill wrapper (batch 3) has NOT been shown to outperform the plain LLM 取象法.** On the 5 batch 3 events scored so far it performs similarly or slightly worse than batch 2. But n=5 is far too small to draw any conclusion — this question won't be answerable until the remaining 25 batch 3 events resolve.

**Caveat on the Apr 15 jump**: The combined p-value dropping from 0.139 → 0.011 is almost entirely driven by new batch 2 events (13 of the 18 new). The full-skill method did not cause this improvement. The honest headline is:

> *"Imagery-based casting in general beats random. Whether the full skill wrapper adds anything on top of just prompting an LLM directly is still an open question."*

## Methodology notes

- Narration phase: blinded narrator (web research for event outcomes; no access to interpretation files during narration)

- Scale: 0–5 qualitative match (0 = no connection, 5 = striking fit)
- Statistical test: Wilcoxon signed-rank, one-tailed, H₁: real > control
- Tie handling: E16 (prior batch) excluded from Wilcoxon; n_effective = 36
