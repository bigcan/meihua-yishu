## Standout hits (new batch)

### E40 — Michael MJ biopic (Real=5, Control=1)
Real hexagram **38 火澤睽 → 10 天澤履 line 5**. 睽 = "opposition, suspicious gaze, fundamental divergence" — directly maps onto MJ's controversial legacy. Line 5 reads *"悔亡，厥宗噬膚，往何咎"* — *regret vanishes, the clan bites through the skin, what blame in advancing?* Audience embraces him through the controversy. Transformation to 履 (treading on a tiger's tail without being bitten). Real event: $97M domestic / $217M global, **biggest biopic opening ever**, smashing Straight Outta Compton's record. The interpretation literally said *"applause following a high-wire act, exhilarating, slightly breathless."*

### E48 — London Marathon (Real=5, Control=1)
Real hexagram **16 雷地豫 → 35 火地晉 line 6**. The transformation invokes 晉's classic image: *"康侯用錫馬蕃庶，**晝日三接**"* — a vassal lord rewarded with horses, **received three times in a single day**. Real event: **three women** finished under 2:16 in the same race — the first time ever — while Sabastian Sawe became the first man to break 2 hours officially (1:59:30 WR). The "thunder over earth = vast crowd in shared rhythm" matches mass-marathon staging; the "sunrise of public recognition" matches the historic breakthrough.

### E60 — SpaceX Starship Flight 12 V3 (Real=5, Control=2)
Real hexagram **14 火天大有 → 26 山天大畜 line 4**. The line text is *"匪其彭，无咎"* — do not boast at the moment of great possession, no disaster. Transformation to 大畜 (Great Accumulation): *"strategic storage and preparation,"* *"yoking the bull before the horns grow."* Real event: launch did **not** happen — only static fire tests. Booster 19 cleared its 33-engine static fire on Apr 15 (literally fire above heaven). Launch deferred to May 2026. The interpretation explicitly described *"the spectacular display deferred for disciplined containment."* This is the second time (after E32) where event drift accidentally vindicated a hexagram about restraint.

### E46 — NFL Draft First Round (Real=5, Control=2)
Real hexagram **17 澤雷隨 → 25 天雷无妄 line 6**. The line text is *"拘係之，乃從維之。王用亨于西山"* — *binding and tethering, then securing and maintaining; the king makes offerings at the western mountain.* Real event: NFL Draft is literally a binding ritual where players are formally "bound and tethered" to teams via a commissioner's announcement on stage. Transformation to 无妄 (Innocence) introduces *"无妄之災"* — calamities that strike despite no wrongdoing — i.e. the bust risk inherent in every pick. Both mechanical and metaphoric fit.

## Standout miss

### E45 — Google Cloud Next (Real=1, Control=5)
This is the largest single-event miss in the entire experiment. The real hexagram (6 天水訟 / Conflict, with line 1's "abandon dispute early") was tonally wrong for a confident product launch. The **control** hexagram drawn at random — **26 山天大畜 → 11 地天泰 line 6** — happens to be one of the most auspicious transitions in the Yijing, with the line text *"何天之衢，亨"* — *"what a highway of heaven, success!"* The Google keynote was explicitly themed around the **"agentic cloud highway"** and the *release of stored TPU/Gemini infrastructure*. The control got a near-perfect pull from random space.

This is honest noise: across 60+ random draws, occasional eerie coincidences are expected. It is the same risk the experiment was designed to measure.
